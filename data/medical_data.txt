Medical Emergency Information

First Aid Procedures:
- Check for responsiveness
- Call emergency services (911/112)
- Perform CPR if trained
- Control bleeding with direct pressure
- Keep victim warm and comfortable

Common Medical Emergencies:
- Heart attack: Chest pain, shortness of breath, nausea
- Stroke: Facial drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulties
- Choking: Inability to speak, cough, or breathe
- Severe bleeding: Apply direct pressure, elevate if possible
- Burns: Cool with water, remove jewelry, cover loosely

Emergency Contact Numbers:
- Emergency Services: 911 (US) / 112 (Europe)
- Poison Control: 1-800-222-1222
- National Suicide Prevention: 988

Important Notes:
- Always call emergency services for life-threatening situations
- Do not move someone with suspected spinal injury
- Keep emergency contact information accessible
- Learn basic first aid and CPR
